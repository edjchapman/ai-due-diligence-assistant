# Helios Robotics Corp. — FY2025 Form 10-K (excerpt)

## Note 2. Going Concern

The Company has incurred recurring operating losses and negative cash flows from
operations since inception, and had an accumulated deficit of $214.6 million as
of December 31, 2025. Based on current forecasts, existing cash and cash
equivalents are expected to fund operations only into the fourth quarter of 2026.
These conditions raise **substantial doubt about the Company's ability to
continue as a going concern** within one year after the date these financial
statements are issued. Management's plans to alleviate this doubt include raising
additional capital and reducing discretionary spending; however, there can be no
assurance such plans will be successful.

## Note 14. Related-Party Transactions

The Company leases its headquarters and principal manufacturing facility in Reno,
Nevada from Vega Holdings LLC, an entity controlled by our Chief Executive
Officer and co-founder, Dr. Ana Vega. Rent expense under this lease was
$1.4 million for the year ended December 31, 2025 (2024: $1.3 million). The lease
was not negotiated at arm's length. In addition, during 2025 the Company obtained
$3.0 million of bridge financing from Vega Holdings LLC bearing interest at 9% per
annum.

## Item 1A. Risk Factors — Customer Concentration

No single customer accounted for more than 10% of revenue in fiscal 2025.
