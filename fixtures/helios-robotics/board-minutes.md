# Helios Robotics Corp. — Minutes of the Board of Directors

**Date:** 28 February 2025 · **Present:** full Board (Dr. Ana Vega recused from Item 2); CFO in attendance.

## 1. Liquidity and Going-Concern Assessment

The CFO reviewed the cash runway, confirming that on current forecasts the
Company's cash funds operations only into Q4 2026. The Board acknowledged that
the auditors are likely to include a going-concern emphasis-of-matter paragraph
in their report. The Board directed management to accelerate the Series D raise
and to prepare a contingency cost-reduction plan.

## 2. Related-Party Lease and Bridge Financing

Dr. Vega recused herself. The Board considered the renewal of the Reno facility
lease with Vega Holdings LLC (an entity controlled by Dr. Vega) at $1.4 million
per annum, and the $3.0 million bridge loan from the same entity at 9% interest.
The independent directors noted the lease was not negotiated at arm's length and
directed that an independent valuation be obtained before the next renewal. The
transactions were **approved by the independent directors** as being in the
Company's interest given current liquidity constraints.

## 3. Other Business

None.
