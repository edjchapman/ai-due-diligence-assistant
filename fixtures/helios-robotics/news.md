# Helios Robotics races to shore up cash as going-concern flag looms

_Automation Report — March 2025_

Helios Robotics Corp. faces a tightening cash runway, with its most recent filing
warning of "substantial doubt" about the company's ability to continue as a going
concern absent a fresh capital raise. Management says existing cash lasts only
into late 2026.

The filing also drew attention to the company's reliance on entities tied to
founder and CEO Dr. Ana Vega. Helios leases its Reno headquarters from a
Vega-controlled entity and recently took a $3 million bridge loan from the same
party. Governance observers said the related-party arrangements, while disclosed
and approved by independent directors, warrant close monitoring.

Helios said it is pursuing a Series D round and expects to provide an update in
the second half of the year.
