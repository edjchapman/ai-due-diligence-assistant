# Meridian Foods to open Midlands distribution centre amid steady growth

_Grocery Trade Journal — February 2025_

Meridian Foods PLC has announced plans for a new chilled-distribution centre in
the Midlands, funded from operating cash flow, as the food group reported
mid-single-digit revenue growth for the year.

Analysts describe Meridian as a steady, diversified operator, noting its customer
base spans grocery retailers, food-service distributors, and wholesalers with no
outsized dependence on any single account. The company's long-standing auditor,
Kingsbridge, issued a clean opinion on the latest accounts.

"There are no obvious red flags here," said one consumer-sector analyst. "It's a
boringly well-run business, which in this market is a compliment."
