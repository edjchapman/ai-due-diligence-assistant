# Meridian Foods PLC — Minutes of the Board of Directors

**Date:** 09 January 2025 · **Present:** full Board; CFO and Company Secretary in attendance.

## 1. Audit

The Audit Committee Chair confirmed that Kingsbridge LLP would be recommended for
reappointment at the AGM, having served since 2019, and reported no disagreements
and an expected unqualified opinion. The Board noted the recommendation.

## 2. Trading Update and Going Concern

The CFO presented a trading update showing revenue growth of 6% and a diversified
customer base with no single customer above 8% of revenue. The Board confirmed it
was satisfied that the going-concern basis remained appropriate.

## 3. Expansion

The Board approved capital expenditure for a new chilled-distribution centre in
the Midlands, funded from operating cash flow.

## 4. Related Parties

The Company Secretary confirmed there were no new related-party transactions to
report beyond director remuneration.
