# Northwind's Pallas dependence draws scrutiny as auditor changes hands

_Composite Materials Weekly — April 2025_

Northwind Materials Inc. is under renewed investor scrutiny after its latest
annual filing confirmed that a single customer, Pallas Automotive Group, drove
close to two-thirds of the company's revenue last year. Analysts note the share
has climbed steadily from around half of revenue in 2023.

"A supply agreement that expires in 2026 with no volume floor is exactly the kind
of thing that keeps a CFO awake," one sell-side analyst commented. Northwind's
management has said it is pursuing a diversification plan.

Separately, Northwind confirmed it replaced its long-standing auditor, Barrow &
Finch, with Crestline Audit early in the year. The company characterised the
change as the result of a routine competitive tender and said there were no
disagreements with the departing firm.
