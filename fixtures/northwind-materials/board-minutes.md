# Northwind Materials Inc. — Minutes of the Board of Directors

**Date:** 12 March 2025 · **Present:** full Board; CFO and General Counsel in attendance.

## 1. Auditor Transition

The Chair of the Audit Committee reported that, following a competitive tender,
the Committee had dismissed Barrow & Finch LLP and appointed Crestline Audit LLP
as independent auditor effective 3 February 2025. The Committee confirmed there
had been no disagreements with the outgoing auditor. The Board **ratified** the
appointment.

## 2. Customer Concentration

The CFO presented an analysis showing that Pallas Automotive Group now accounts
for roughly 62% of net revenue. The Board discussed the strategic risk of this
dependence, noting the master supply agreement lapses in December 2026 with no
minimum-volume commitment. Management was directed to present a customer-
diversification plan at the next meeting and to open commercial discussions with
at least two prospective large accounts.

## 3. Other Business

Approval of the FY2026 capital budget was deferred pending the diversification plan.
